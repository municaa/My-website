Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

How often should I groom my pet?
The frequency of grooming depends on your pet's breed, coat type, and lifestyle. Generally:

• Short-haired breeds: Every 4-8 weeks
• Long-haired breeds: Every 2-6 weeks
• Regular brushing at home is recommended between professional grooming sessions
What services are included in basic grooming?
Our basic grooming package includes:

• Bath with premium shampoo
• Blow dry and brush out
• Nail trimming
• Ear cleaning
• Sanitary trim

Appointments & Pricing

How long does a grooming session take?
A typical grooming session takes 2-3 hours, depending on your pet's size, coat condition, and the services requested. We prioritize quality and your pet's comfort over speed.
Do you accept walk-ins?
While we do accept walk-ins based on availability, we highly recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure we can accommodate you at your preferred time.

Pet Care & Safety

Are your groomers certified?
Yes, all our groomers are professionally certified and have extensive experience in pet grooming. They regularly attend training sessions to stay updated with the latest grooming techniques and safety practices.
What precautions do you take for anxious pets?
We understand that some pets may feel anxious during grooming. Our staff is trained to handle nervous pets with extra care and patience. We use calming techniques and can adjust the grooming process to make your pet feel more comfortable.
