Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a grooming session take?
The duration varies depending on the package and your pet's needs. Basic grooming typically takes 1-2 hours, while luxury treatments may take 2-3 hours.
What's included in the basic package?
Our Basic Pawsome package includes bath, blow dry, brush out, nail trimming, ear cleaning, and basic hygiene trim.
Do you cater to all breeds?
Yes, we have experience with all dog and cat breeds. Our groomers are trained to handle different coat types and temperaments.
How often should I groom my pet?
We recommend professional grooming every 4-8 weeks, depending on your pet's coat type and lifestyle. We can create a personalized grooming schedule during your visit.
