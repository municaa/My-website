Meet the Designer

Monica Angela L. Fernando

Web Designer

While making a website about pet grooming services for cats and dogs, I was able to show not just what I learned in web design, but also how much I love animals. I care a lot about the health and cleanliness of pets, so I wanted to create something helpful for pet owners.

This website also shows how much I love cats and dogs—they feel like family to me. Through this project, I hope people see that pets deserve to be loved, cared for, and treated well.

Also, this activity helped me become more comfortable with typing code and understanding how browsers read and display it. It also made me more patient and detail-oriented because even small mistakes can affect the whole layout. It was a useful experience that gave me a good foundation for learning more about web development
